Welcome To Namma Chennai (Sitecore User Group)

 

Sitecore User Group Chennai is a group of like minded people sharing their sitecore experience with the community! Our objective is to promote sitecore community by organizing host meetups and webinars at regular intervals! This is a group for everyone  

and is not restricted to any specific geographic location and knowledge-be it Novice to Expert on sitecore.

Register yourself today and be part of a sitecore wave in Namma Chennai.

If you are interested in presenting a topic or sponsoring us for a meetup, do reach out to us!

About Us

We are a group of people who enjoy working on sitecore and are trying to build a community in Chennai where sitecore enthusiast’s can meet, share experiences, learn collaboratively and socialize. We have just started this community and would love to have you associated with us. Register Now!!
